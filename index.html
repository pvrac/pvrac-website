About Us

Welcome to Pierre van Ryneveld Athletics Club, where passion meets pavement and every stride takes you higher. We're more than just a running club—we're a community of athletes who believe in pushing boundaries, supporting each other, and celebrating every kilometer.

Whether you're a seasoned marathoner or just starting your running journey, you'll find your pace with us. Our diverse group of runners welcomes all abilities and ages, united by our love for the sport and the open road.

How to Join

  • Register on the ASA website
    Choose: Athletics Gauteng North as province and Pierre van Ryneveld as club
  • Complete the ASA 2026 License form
    Official Athletics South Africa registration document
  • Complete the PVR AC membership form
    Club-specific application with WhatsApp group preferences
  • Email forms + proof of payment to:
    pvrsportsclub@gmail.com
    ⚠️ Payment proof is required - application won't be processed without it
